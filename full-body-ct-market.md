<p><p>The full body CT market comprises primarily two types of imaging technologies: CT scanners and PET-CT scanners. CT scanners utilize X-ray technology to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions. PET-CT scanners combine positron emission tomography and computed tomography, offering metabolic and anatomical information, which enhances the accuracy of tumor detection and monitoring. Both market segments are integral in enhancing diagnostic capabilities, driving advancements in patient care and treatment planning.</p></p>
