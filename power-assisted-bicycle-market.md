<p><strong>The Power Assisted Bicycle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mid-Drive</li><li>Hub-Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power-assisted bicycle market primarily features two types: mid-drive and hub-drive systems. Mid-drive motors are located at the bike's cranks, providing better weight distribution and efficiency, making them ideal for steep terrains and off-road cycling. Hub-drive systems place the motor in the wheel hub, offering simpler maintenance and a more straightforward installation. These bikes excel in urban commuting and flat terrains. Both types cater to different rider preferences, balancing performance, ease of use, and versatility.</p></p>
