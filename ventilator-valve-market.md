<p><p>The ventilator valve market comprises several types, including solenoid valves, proportional valves, and others. Solenoid valves provide quick on/off control for precise airflow management, making them ideal for rapid response in ventilators. Proportional valves, on the other hand, allow for fine-tuned adjustments to airflow and pressure, ensuring optimal patient support. Other types may include check valves and pressure relief valves, each serving specialized roles in enhancing the efficiency and safety of ventilation systems in medical applications.</p></p>
