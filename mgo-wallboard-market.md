<p><p>The MgO wallboard market can be categorized into three types based on thickness: thin, medium, and thick. Thin wallboards, less than 8 mm, are often used for interior applications due to their lightweight nature. Medium wallboards, ranging from 8 to 15 mm, offer a balance of strength and flexibility, making them suitable for both walls and ceilings. Thick wallboards, exceeding 15 mm, provide superior durability and insulation, making them ideal for high-impact or exterior applications. Each type serves specific construction needs effectively.</p></p>
