<p><p>The anti-bedsore mattress market is categorized into two main types: static and dynamic. Static anti-bedsore mattresses maintain a consistent surface to provide pressure relief through their design, often using foam or gel materials. In contrast, dynamic anti-bedsore mattresses utilize air or fluid systems that actively change pressure points, promoting blood circulation and further preventing sores. Both types are essential in healthcare settings, catering to patients at risk of pressure ulcers due to prolonged immobility.</p></p>
