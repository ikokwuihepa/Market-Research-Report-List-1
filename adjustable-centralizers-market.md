<p><p>Adjustable centralizers are specialized tools used in the oil and gas industry to ensure that pipes, casings, or drilling assemblies remain centered within a borehole. These devices are designed to be adjustable, allowing for customizable positioning to accommodate varying borehole dimensions and conditions. Their primary function is to maintain proper spacing during cementing operations, which is crucial for preventing casing damage and ensuring well integrity.</p><p>The Adjustable Centralizers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period, driven by increasing drilling activities, particularly in offshore oil exploration and production.
