Catalase is essential for eliminating hydrogen peroxide, facilitating eco-friendly bleaching processes. Pectinase breaks down pectin in natural fibers, improving dye uptake and fabric quality. Other enzymes cater to specific processes, enhancing efficiency and sustainability in textile manufacturing.</p></p>
