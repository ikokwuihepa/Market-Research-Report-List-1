<p><p>Hybrid geothermal heat pumps combine traditional geothermal systems with supplemental heating methods for enhanced efficiency. In the single-stage type, the system operates with one compressor and is suitable for moderate heating demands. The two-stage type, on the other hand, features dual compressors that provide better performance and efficiency, particularly in extreme temperature conditions. This two-stage approach allows for more versatility in energy usage, making it ideal for larger or more variable heating needs. Both types cater to diverse residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
