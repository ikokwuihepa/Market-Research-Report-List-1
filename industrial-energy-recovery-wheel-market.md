<p><strong>The Industrial Energy Recovery Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Energy Recovery Wheel market comprises three main types: Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. Condensation Rotors capture and transfer latent heat from exhaust air, enhancing energy efficiency. Hygroscopic Rotors absorb moisture from the air, recovering energy while also controlling humidity levels in the environment. Sorption Rotors combine these principles, using advanced materials to simultaneously manage heat and moisture. Each type serves various industrial applications, optimizing energy use and contributing to sustainability goals.</p></p>
