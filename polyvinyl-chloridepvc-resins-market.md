<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) resins market consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **Suspension PVC Resins** are produced through a suspension polymerization process, offering high clarity and rigidity, commonly used in pipes and fittings. </p><p>2. **Emulsion PVC Resins** are generated via emulsion polymerization, providing excellent impact resistance and flexibility, suitable for coatings and adhesives. </p><p>3. **Blended PVC Resins** combine various PVC types, enhancing properties such as processability and durability, making them ideal for a wide range of applications, including films and sheets.</p></p>
