<p><p>The Total Suspended Solids (TSS) monitor market primarily consists of two types: submersible probes and insert probes. Submersible probes are designed for direct deployment in water bodies, providing real-time measurements of TSS levels in various aquatic environments. In contrast, insert probes are installed within a pipeline or treatment system, allowing for continuous monitoring of suspended solids in flowing water. Both types are essential for applications in water quality management, wastewater treatment, and environmental monitoring, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
