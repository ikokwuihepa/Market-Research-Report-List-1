<p><p>The high pressure isolation valves market includes several key types: </p><p>- **Ball Valves**: Featuring a rotating ball to control flow, they offer quick shut-off and low pressure drop.</p><p>- **Gate Valves**: Used for on/off control, these valves provide minimal resistance when fully opened.</p><p>- **Butterfly Valves**: Utilizing a disc to regulate flow, they are compact and suitable for large volume applications.</p><p>- **Globe Valves**: Known for precise flow control, they have a spherical body and are ideal for throttling.</p><p>- **Others**: This category encompasses specialized valves designed for unique applications.</p></p>
