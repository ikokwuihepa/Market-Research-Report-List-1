<p><strong>The VoIP Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-band</li><li>Dual-band</li><li>Tri-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VoIP router market is categorized into single-band, dual-band, and tri-band types, each offering different frequencies for data transmission. Single-band routers operate solely on the 2.4 GHz frequency, suitable for basic tasks but limited in speed and interference. Dual-band routers utilize both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing enhanced performance and reduced congestion. Tri-band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, optimizing performance for multiple devices and heavy bandwidth usage, making them ideal for high-demand environments.</p></p>
