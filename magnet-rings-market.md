<p><p>Magnet rings are categorized into two primary market types: radial and axial. Radial magnet rings are designed to attract or repel forces perpendicular to the central axis, commonly used in applications like electric motors. In contrast, axial magnet rings have magnetic fields aligned along the axis, optimizing force in applications such as generators and sensors. Both types serve specialized roles in various industries, enhancing performance and efficiency based on their unique magnetic orientations and configurations.</p></p>
