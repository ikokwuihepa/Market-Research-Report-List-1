<p><p>Cerebral Spinal Fluid (CSF) shunt systems are vital for managing conditions like hydrocephalus by directing excess fluid from the brain. The market is categorized into fixed valves and adjustable valves. Fixed valves offer a consistent pressure flow, ensuring reliable drainage but limited customization. In contrast, adjustable valves allow for post-implantation pressure modifications, catering to individual patient needs and fluctuations in intracranial pressure. Together, these systems enhance patient outcomes by providing tailored solutions for CSF management.</p></p>
