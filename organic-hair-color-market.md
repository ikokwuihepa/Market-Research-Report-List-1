<p><p>The organic hair color market primarily consists of two types: permanent and temporary hair color. Permanent hair color is formulated to provide long-lasting effects, penetrating the hair shaft for durability and vibrant coverage, often used for gray coverage and vivid shades. In contrast, temporary hair color offers a short-term solution, coating the hair's surface without penetrating, allowing for easy application and removal. This type is popular for those looking to experiment with different looks without commitment.</p></p>
