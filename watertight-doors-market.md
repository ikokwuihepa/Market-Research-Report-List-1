<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Watertight Doors</li><li>Electric Watertight Doors</li><li>Pneumatic Watertight Doors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The watertight doors market comprises three primary types based on their operation: hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic. Hydraulic watertight doors utilize fluid pressure for controlled movement, ensuring durability and robust sealing in marine and industrial applications. Electric watertight doors are powered by electric motors, offering quick operation and advanced automation features. Pneumatic watertight doors use compressed air to open and close, benefiting industries where rapid deployment and space-saving designs are essential. Each type serves critical functions in flood prevention and safety regulation.</p></p>
