<p><p>The marine electric motor market comprises two primary types: electric inboard motors and electric outboard motors. Electric inboard motors are integrated within the hull of boats, offering efficient power for larger vessels and enabling smooth operation with reduced noise and emissions. In contrast, electric outboard motors are mounted externally, providing maneuverability and versatility for smaller boats and personal watercraft. Both types are increasingly favored for their environmental benefits, performance efficiency, and advancements in battery technology, driving growth in the maritime sector.</p></p>
