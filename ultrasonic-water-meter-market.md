<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Water Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic water meter market can be categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I meters utilize transit-time technology, measuring the time difference of ultrasonic signals traveling with and against the water flow, ensuring high accuracy and low maintenance. Type II meters, on the other hand, employ Doppler technology, which gauges the frequency shift of reflected signals from particles in the water, making them suitable for wastewater and dirty water applications. Both types cater to varying measurement needs.</p></p>
