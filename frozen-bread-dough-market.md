<p><strong>The Frozen Bread Dough Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermented</li><li>Unfermented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frozen bread dough market is divided into two primary types: fermented and unfermented. Fermented dough is pre-proofed, allowing for flavor development and improved texture upon baking. This type requires less final proofing time, making it convenient for bakeries seeking efficiency. Unfermented dough, on the other hand, is not proofed before freezing, requiring longer final proofing once defrosted. This type often appeals to establishments desiring control over the fermentation process, resulting in a fresher taste and customizable texture.</p></p>
