<p><ul><li>Multi-leaf Spring</li><li>Mono-leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive leaf spring assembly market is primarily categorized into two types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of several stacked layers, offering enhanced load-bearing capacity and durability, making them suitable for heavy vehicles like trucks and SUVs. In contrast, mono-leaf springs use a single, broader leaf that provides a lighter, more compact design, ideal for smaller vehicles and those where weight reduction is crucial. Both types play essential roles in vehicle suspension systems, improving handling and comfort.</p></p>
