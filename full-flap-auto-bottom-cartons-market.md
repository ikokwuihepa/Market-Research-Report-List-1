<p><p>Full flap auto bottom cartons are packaging solutions that feature a design where the base folds down automatically, providing enhanced stability and ease of assembly. Glued cartons, on the other hand, utilize adhesive to secure the base, allowing for quicker assembly and increased durability, catering to high-volume production needs and more robust applications.</p></p>
