<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) tracker systems are designed to enhance solar energy capture by orienting solar panels towards the sun throughout the day. Single-axis trackers move along one axis, typically east to west, optimizing energy collection during peak sunlight hours. Dual-axis trackers offer greater flexibility by adjusting both vertically and horizontally, maximizing exposure across varying sun angles and optimizing efficiency year-round. Both systems help improve energy output, making them increasingly popular in the growing solar market.</p></p>
