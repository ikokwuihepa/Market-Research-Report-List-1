<p><p>The Polyester TPU Films market is categorized into three main types based on breathability: Low, Medium, and High Breathable TPU films. Low Breathable TPU films offer minimal ventilation, making them ideal for applications requiring moisture retention. Medium Breathable TPU films strike a balance, providing moderate airflow suitable for varied uses. High Breathable TPU films maximize ventilation, making them perfect for applications where moisture management and comfort are critical, such as in outdoor gear and medical textiles.</p></p>
