<p><p>The Polymer Prefilled Syringe market offers different types based on material composition. Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) is known for its high clarity, excellent barrier properties, and low extractables, making it suitable for sensitive medications. Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) combines the benefits of polymers and provides improved mechanical strength and chemical resistance, enhancing stability. Other materials may include various plastics and composites, each selected based on specific requirements like flexibility, safety, and compatibility with pharmaceutical substances.</p></p>
