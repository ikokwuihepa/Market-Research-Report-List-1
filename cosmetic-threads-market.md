<p><p>The cosmetic threads market is categorized into three main types: barb & cone threads, screw threads, and smooth threads. Barb & cone threads feature barbs that anchor into the skin, providing greater lift and support. Screw threads utilize a spiral design that aids in tissue tightening and volumization. Smooth threads, lacking barbs, offer minimal lifting but stimulate collagen production for subtle rejuvenation. Each thread type serves distinct purposes in non-surgical aesthetic procedures, catering to varying patient needs in facial rejuvenation.</p></p>
