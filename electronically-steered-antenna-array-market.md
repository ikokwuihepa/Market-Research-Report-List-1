<p><p>The Electronically Steered Antenna Array (ESAA) market can be divided into two main types: active and passive arrays. Active arrays have transmit and receive modules at each element, allowing for dynamic beamforming and improved performance in electronic warfare and radar applications. In contrast, passive arrays rely on external components for beam steering and are typically simpler and more cost-effective. Both types are essential for modern communication, defense, and satellite systems, catering to different performance and budget requirements.</p></p>
