<p><p>Polyaspartic garage floor coatings are divided into two primary types: pure coatings and hybrid coatings. Pure coatings consist of 100% polyaspartic resins, offering superior durability, rapid curing times, and excellent resistance to chemicals and UV degradation. In contrast, hybrid coatings typically combine polyaspartic resins with other materials, such as epoxy, enhancing flexibility and adhesion while providing some cost advantages. Both types are popular for their ability to enhance the aesthetic appeal and longevity of garage floors.</p></p>
