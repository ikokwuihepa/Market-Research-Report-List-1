<p><strong>The Coolant Chillers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Type</li><li>Water-Cooled Type</li><li>Evaporative Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coolant chillers market encompasses three primary types: air-cooled, water-cooled, and evaporative chillers. </p><p>Air-cooled chillers utilize ambient air for cooling, making them efficient for smaller applications and areas with limited water supply. Water-cooled chillers, on the other hand, rely on water from cooling towers, offering greater efficiency and capacity for larger industrial settings. Meanwhile, evaporative chillers combine air and water cooling, leveraging evaporation to enhance efficiency, making them suitable for diverse industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
