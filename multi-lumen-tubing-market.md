<p><p>The multi-lumen tubing market consists of various types, each suited for specific applications in medical and industrial fields. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its chemical resistance and high temperature tolerance. ePTFE (expanded PTFE) offers improved flexibility and porosity. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) combines chemical resistance with transparency, while PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) provides high thermal stability and flexibility. PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) is recognized for its strength and biocompatibility. Other materials may include silicone or nylon, catering to diverse requirements.</p></p>
