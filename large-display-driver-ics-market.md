<p><p>The large display driver IC market is primarily segmented into two types: Source Display Driver ICs and Gate Display Driver ICs. Source Display Driver ICs are responsible for controlling the pixel data and driving the output, ensuring picture quality and color accuracy. In contrast, Gate Display Driver ICs manage the timing and control of pixel row scanning, facilitating the sequential activation of rows. Together, these components are crucial in enhancing display performance in various applications, including TVs, monitors, and smartphones.</p></p>
