<p><p>The steel piles market is primarily categorized into large diameter and micro piles. Large diameter piles are designed to support heavy loads and are often used in deep foundations for structures like bridges and high-rise buildings. They offer stability and enhanced load-bearing capacity. Micro piles, on the other hand, are smaller in diameter and suitable for confined spaces, often used in retrofitting existing structures or in areas with challenging soil conditions. Both types enhance structural integrity and support specialized construction needs.</p></p>
