<p><p>The Outdoor Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) market is divided into two primary types: Vision-Based and LiDAR+Vision Based. Vision-Based AMRs rely on cameras and computer vision techniques to navigate and understand their environment. They are often more cost-effective but may struggle in low-light conditions. Conversely, LiDAR+Vision Based AMRs combine laser scanning technology with visual data to create detailed 3D maps, enhancing accuracy and reliability in diverse terrains and lighting, making them suitable for more complex outdoor applications.</p></p>
