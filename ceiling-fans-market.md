<p><p>The ceiling fan market is primarily divided into AC and DC ceiling fans. AC ceiling fans are powered by alternating current, offering reliability and simplicity, typically resulting in a higher power consumption. They are widely used in households and commercial spaces. In contrast, DC ceiling fans utilize direct current technology, making them more energy-efficient and quieter in operation. They often feature advanced functionalities like remote controls and variable speed settings, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers seeking modern features.</p></p>
