<p><p>The refractory grade bauxite market is divided into two main types based on the calcining method: shaft furnace calcining bauxite and converter calcining bauxite. Shaft furnace calcining bauxite is processed in vertical shaft furnaces, offering a cost-effective solution with consistent quality and high-temperature resistance. In contrast, converter calcining bauxite utilizes a more advanced process, often resulting in superior material properties such as enhanced purity and thermal stability, making it suitable for high-performance refractory applications in demanding environments.</p></p>
